Drupal Open Days 2014

Dublin, 16-17 May
Drupal Open Days is bringing together Drupal developers, themers, end users and those interested in learning more about Drupal for two days of talks, sessions and collaborative discussions.
Register

Registration is free thanks to our sponsors

Featured Sessions

Dublin Business Innovation Centre presentation

The Guinness Enterprise Centre is a dynamic community of high potential businesses. The centre is a hub of entrepreneurial enterprises and business and investment support services.

Keynote: Live Free or Die Hard

This years keynote will be given by Dr. Mihai Bilauca and James Sweeney. The talk will cover Limerick City and County Councils use of open source based technologies to remove vendor dependencies and deliver value to its users.

A 3rd level institute's experience with using Drupal as a development platform

GMIT's Martin Gibbons will give a presentation on how they have used Drupal to migrate some of their custom web sites, build new websites, and eventually have all web presences utilising Drupal's Open Source platform.

There will be time for Q&A at the end.
